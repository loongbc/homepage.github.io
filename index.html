About Me

Hello! I am Baichao Long, a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Tongji University. I hold both a Bachelor's and Master's degree from the University of Shanghai for Science and Technology, where I focused on intelligent transportation and smart cities. My research interests lie at the intersection of cutting-edge technologies, including multimodal learning, visual content creation and manipulation, autonomous driving, embodied AI, and large language models (LLMs). I am passionate about developing innovative solutions that enhance urban mobility and create smarter, more connected cities. In addition to my core research, I have ventured into interdisciplinary projects that merge engineering with medicine, such as addressing "Congenital Tracheal Stenosis in Children." This work has broadened my perspective on how technology can positively impact human health. I have also been fortunate to receive several recognitions, including a national first prize in mathematical modeling. Beyond academia, I enjoy playing basketball and running, which help me stay active and focused. Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to connecting with you!
